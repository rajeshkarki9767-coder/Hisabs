Privacy Policy

How Hisabs handles your data — plainly and honestly.

What we store, and where

Hisabs stores your data in two places: your browser (for fast local access while you work) and your cloud database (so it's safe and syncs across your devices).

Every business, book, entry, party, category, account, and staff invite you create is kept in both places. When you make a change, it saves locally instantly and uploads to the cloud within a few seconds. The small status pill at the top of the page shows whether sync is up to date.

What's stored in the cloud

  • Your sign-in email (for authentication; the password is never stored in plain text — it is hashed)
  • A user profile with your display name and, if you set one, your profile picture (stored as a small compressed image)
  • Every business you own, plus its books, entries, parties, categories, accounts, account groups, and audit expenses
  • Any optional contact details you add to a party — phone numbers, email addresses, date of birth, and free-form notes
  • Per-business preferences such as the currency and default phone country code
  • Team invitations you send and receive — including the inviter's display name and the business name shown on the invite card

The party contact details above are entirely optional — a party with no phone, email, DOB, or notes stores none of them. You can edit or clear them at any time, and deleting a party removes its contact details with it.

What stays in your browser only

  • Your appearance preferences (theme, accent color, period filter)
  • UI state like which view you were on, scroll position, draft entry text
  • A device identifier used to deduplicate sync events (random, not linked to you)

Who can see your data

In the cloud, row-level security means only you can read your business data. The owner of a business can see all its data; invited team members can see the businesses they've accepted into; nobody else can read it.

What we don't do

  • No advertising
  • No analytics or telemetry — we don't track your usage
  • No selling, sharing, or transferring data to anyone
  • No third-party tracking pixels, cookies, or fingerprinting
  • No cross-site tracking — Hisabs runs only on its own domain

Cookies and storage

Hisabs uses your browser's localStorage (not cookies) to keep you signed in and to cache your data for fast access. None of these are tracking cookies — they're functional storage that the app needs to work.

Deletion

When you delete a record (business, account, entry, etc.), it is permanently removed from the cloud database within a few seconds. There is no soft-delete or trash bin. Deletion cascades: deleting a business removes all its books, entries, and associated data in one transaction.

To delete everything, including your account: Settings → Data → Danger zone → Delete all my data and sign out. This removes every business you own from the cloud, removes your profile, deletes your sign-in account, and signs you out.

Backups you control

  • Settings → Data → Backup everything downloads a JSON file of all your businesses
  • Per business → Backup & restore downloads one business at a time
  • Backup files contain your data but not your password

Hosting

The Hisabs app is served as a static web page. The hosting provider can see your IP and visit time (standard for any website) but cannot see the data inside Hisabs — that's only visible to the database under your account.

Children

Hisabs is bookkeeping software for adults running businesses, and our Terms require users to be at least 18. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has signed up, contact support.

Changes to this policy

If Hisabs's data handling changes meaningfully, this policy will be updated.

Contact

Questions about your privacy or data? Contact us at the support email listed in the app's Settings page.
